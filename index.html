Diversity & Inclusion

Diversity

Diversity refers to the presence of differences within a given setting. In the workplace, that can mean differences in race, ethnicity, gender, gender identity, sexual orientation, age, and socioeconomic background.

Diversity is important because it brings different perspectives to the table, which can lead to more innovative solutions and better decision-making. It also helps to create a more inclusive and equitable environment.
